Name: Cheesy Spinach Omelette.
Ingredients: Eggs, Cheddar cheese, Fresh Spinach, Olive oil, salt, pepper.
Instructions: 1. Beat eggs in a bowl. 2. Heat olive oil in a pan. 3. Add fresh spinach and saute. 4. Pour beaten eggs into the pan. 5. Add cheddar cheese, salt, and peper. 6. Cook until the omelette is set.

Name: Avocado and Egg Toast.
Ingredients: Eggs, Avocado, Bread, Salt, Pepper.
Instructions: 1. Fry the Eggs. 2. Mash avocado onto toasted bread. 3. Place fried eggs on top of the avocado toast. 4. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Ingredients: Rice vinegar, white sugar, fresh cilantro leaves, mayonaise, boneless chicken breast.
